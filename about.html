What exactly is Dietbook?

Dietbook is a diet recommendation, and record keeping site. This site has been developed by students of Xavier Institute of Engineering, Mahim; as the mini-project for group eight of secomps. We aim to considerably reduce the time and efforts required in tailoring a user-specific diet, according to their fitness goals, for the betterment of health.

How does it work?

We take a look at your physical details and dietary goals, and then prepare a meal plan for you according to your dietary preferences. That's not it! We keep a record of your physical details (which remain private) so that you can come back and update them, and receive a new diet. With our system, your good health is assured!
